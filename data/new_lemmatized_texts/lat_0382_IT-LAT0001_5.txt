 sepelio que is in finis possessio suus in Thamnathsara qui sino sum in mons Ephraim ab septentrionalis pars mons Gaas 
 seruio que Israel Dominus cunctus dies Iosue et senium qui longus uiuo tempus post Iosue et qui nosco omnis opus Dominus qui facio in Israel 
 os quoque Ioseph qui fero filius Israel de Aegyptus sepelio in Sychem in pars ager qui emo Iacob ab filius Emmor pater Sychem centum nouellus ouis et sum in possessio filius Ioseph 
 Eleazar quoque filius Aaron morior sum et sepelio is in Gaab Fineesus filius is qui datum sum is in mons Ephraim
